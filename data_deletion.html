User Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to request the deletion of your data from the FOCUS app, please follow the steps below:

  1. Open the FOCUS app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the settings menu.
  3. Locate the "Delete My Data" option.
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm the deletion of your data.

If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, please contact our support team at focus.app.help@gmail.com.
